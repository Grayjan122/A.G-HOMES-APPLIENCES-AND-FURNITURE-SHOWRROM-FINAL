# 📊 Inventory Manager Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 What's New?

The Inventory Manager now has a **powerful dashboard** with:
- 📈 Real-time inventory statistics
- 📊 Visual charts (stock levels & request status)
- 🚚 Upcoming deliveries tracking
- 📝 Recent requests monitoring
- ⚠️ Low stock alerts

---

## ✨ Key Features at a Glance

### 6 Statistics Cards
1. **Total Products** - All products in your inventory
2. **Good Stock** - Products with adequate levels
3. **Low Stock** - Products needing restock
4. **Out of Stock** - Empty products
5. **Pending Requests** - Awaiting approval
6. **Incoming Deliveries** - In transit

### 2 Visual Charts
1. **Stock Level Distribution** - See your inventory health
2. **Request Status Overview** - Track request progress

### 2 Tracking Sections
1. **Upcoming Deliveries** - Monitor deliveries in transit
2. **Recent Requests** - View latest stock requests

## 🎨 What Each Card Shows

### 📦 Total Products
- **What:** Count of all products in your location
- **Color:** Purple/Blue
- **Action:** None (informational)

### ✅ Good Stock
- **What:** Products above reorder level
- **Color:** Green
- **Action:** None (healthy status)

### ⚠️ Low Stock Items
- **What:** Products at or below reorder level
- **Color:** Yellow
- **Action:** Request restock if needed

### 🚫 Out of Stock
- **What:** Products with zero quantity
- **Color:** Red
- **Action:** **URGENT** - Request immediately

### 📝 Pending Requests
- **What:** Stock requests waiting for approval
- **Color:** Cyan
- **Action:** Follow up if delayed

### 🚚 Incoming Deliveries
- **What:** Deliveries currently in transit
- **Color:** Purple/Blue
- **Action:** Prepare to receive

---

## 📊 Understanding the Charts

### Stock Level Distribution (Doughnut)
- **Green** = Good Stock (above reorder level)
- **Yellow** = Low Stock (at reorder level)
- **Red** = Out of Stock (zero quantity)

**Goal:** Keep green section as large as possible!

### Request Status Overview (Doughnut)
- **Orange** = Pending (waiting approval)
- **Blue** = On Going (being prepared)
- **Purple** = On Delivery (in transit)

**Goal:** Minimize pending, maximize deliveries!

## ⚠️ Low Stock Alert

### When It Appears:
- Shows up when ANY product is low on stock
- Yellow warning box at the bottom

### What to Do:
1. Note the number of low stock items
2. Navigate to **Inventory** page
3. Filter by **"Low Stock"**
4. Request restock for critical items
