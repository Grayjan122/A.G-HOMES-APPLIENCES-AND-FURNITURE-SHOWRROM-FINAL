METHOD 1: From Product Grid
----------------------------
1. Go to Products page
2. Hover over any product image
3. See 🔍 zoom icon appear in bottom-right
4. Click the zoom icon
5. ✨ Image opens in full-screen modal!

METHOD 2: From Product Details
-------------------------------
1. Click on any product to view details
2. Scroll to "Preview Image" section
3. See "🔍 Click to zoom" indicator
4. Hover over image (slight zoom effect)
5. Click the image
6. ✨ Image opens in full-screen modal!

CLOSING THE ZOOM MODAL:
-----------------------
- Click the image itself
- Click the X button in top-right
- Press ESC key (Bootstrap default)
