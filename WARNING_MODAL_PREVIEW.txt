COMPARISON: Before vs After Feature
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BEFORE (No Auto Logout):
  User leaves computer
      ↓
  Hours pass
      ↓
  User still logged in ❌
      ↓
  Security risk!
      ↓
  Someone can access their account

AFTER (With Auto Logout):
  User leaves computer
      ↓
  59 minutes pass
      ↓
  Warning appears (but user is away)
      ↓
  60 seconds pass
      ↓
  Auto logout ✅
      ↓
  Account secure!
      ↓
  Must log in again to access
