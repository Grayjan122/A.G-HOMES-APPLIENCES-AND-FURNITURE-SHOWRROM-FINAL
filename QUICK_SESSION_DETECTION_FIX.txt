Browser A: Nothing happens ❌ (still logged in)


🔍 ROOT CAUSE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2. Browser A checked every 10 seconds (too slow!)
3. Browser A missed the brief "Offline" window

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Increased "Offline" delay: 0.5s → 2 seconds
2. Faster checking: 10s → 3 seconds
3. Browser A now detects within 1-3 seconds!
