🌐 CUSTOMER URLS (Public):
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Main Homepage:
   http://localhost:3000/
   → Redirects to SHOP automatically

Shop Page (E-commerce):
   http://localhost:3000/shop
   → Customer can browse products
   → Create account
   → Login
   → Add to cart
   → See inventory by location

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🔐 STAFF/ADMIN URLS (Internal):
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Admin/Staff Login:
   http://localhost:3000/admin-login
   → For Admin, Inventory Manager, Warehouse, Sales Clerk

After Login, staff access their dashboards:
   → Admin: http://localhost:3000/adminPage
   → Inventory Manager: http://localhost:3000/inventoryPage
   → Warehouse Rep: http://localhost:3000/warehousePage
   → Sales Clerk: http://localhost:3000/salesClerkPage

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📊 HOW IT WORKS:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CUSTOMERS:
   1. Visit http://localhost:3000/
   2. Auto-redirected to /shop
   3. Browse products
   4. Create account (ecommerce_customers table)
   5. Shop online

STAFF:
   1. Visit http://localhost:3000/admin-login
   2. Login with staff credentials
   3. Access their respective dashboards
   4. Use POS, inventory management, etc.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🔑 IMPORTANT:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✅ Customer accounts are SEPARATE from staff accounts
✅ Customers use: ecommerce_customers table
✅ Staff use: account table
✅ No conflicts between systems
